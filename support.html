Restore Purchases

  1. Open DesireCalc and go to Settings → Subscription.
  2. Tap Restore Purchases.
  3. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID used to buy the subscription and that you have a stable network connection.

Restoring never charges you again; it only revalidates existing purchases via Apple StoreKit.

Manage or Cancel Subscription

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple account. You can change, cancel, or switch plans at any time:

Troubleshooting

Common fixes

  • Ensure you are online and signed in with the correct Apple ID.
  • From Settings → Subscription, try Restore Purchases again.
  • Kill and relaunch the app after successful restore.
  • If text appears in the wrong language, check the in-app language setting or your system language.
