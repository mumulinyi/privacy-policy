1. Scope

This Policy applies to the DesireCalc mobile application and related services. "Personal information" means data that identifies an individual on its own or when combined with other data. If you do not agree with this Policy, please stop using the app.

2. Information We Collect

a) You Provide

  • Account & Subscription: We process Apple-provided transaction credentials (e.g., receipt data) for purchases. We do not store payment card details.
  • Support: When you contact us, you may provide an email address, issue description, logs, or other content.

b) Generated During Use

  • Usage & Diagnostics: Crash logs and diagnostics to improve stability and security, without sensitive identifiers.
  • Cloud Data (if enabled): App data may be stored in your iCloud/CloudKit account space.

c) AI/Recognition (if applicable)

  • Submitted text/images are used only to perform the requested analysis and return results. We do not use them for model training.
  • If third-party AI is involved (e.g., QWEN), we only transmit necessary data when you trigger those features. See the provider's privacy policy.

3. How We Use Information

  • Provide and maintain core features (recording, analysis, presentation).

  • Improve experience and performance; diagnostics and statistics.
  • Respond to requests; provide support and customer service.
  • Comply with laws and regulatory requirements.

4. Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell personal information. We may share or entrust processing when lawful, legitimate, and necessary:
