DesireCalc

Record, analyze, and understand your expenses — with clear visualizations, helpful insights, and optional AI analysis.

Get DesireCalc on the App Store (Pricing and availability may vary by region.)

Overview

DesireCalc makes everyday expense tracking effortless and insightful. Quickly log items, set categories and impact levels, view timelines and wallets, and see statistics that help you make better decisions. For advanced use, AI-powered recognition and analysis can assist with extracting information and providing smarter summaries.

Key Features

Fast Recording
Quickly add expenses with intuitive inputs.
Categories & Impact Levels
Organize spending and assess impact to your budget.
Currency & Localization
Choose your currency; English and Simplified Chinese supported.
Timeline & Wallet
Review spending history and wallet balances at a glance.
Charts & Statistics
Visualize totals, trends, and category breakdowns.
AI Analysis (Optional)
Recognize receipts/images and generate smart insights.

Why DesireCalc

  • Understand where your money goes and how to optimize.
  • Make informed choices with impact-aware categories.
  • Automated recognition reduces manual input.
  • Designed with privacy in mind — your data stays under your control.

Plans & Pricing

Basic features are free. Premium features (AI Analysis) are available via Monthly and Yearly subscriptions. Pricing is shown by Apple and automatically localized in your currency.

  • Manage subscriptions from your Apple account: Apple Support — Manage Subscriptions
  • Request refunds via Apple: reportaproblem.apple.com

Availability

  • Platforms: iPhone/iPad
  • Recommended: iOS 15 or later
  • Languages: English, Simplified Chinese
